About Petopia

The Pet Express website is part of the award-winning pet shop group based in South West England.

It all started back in 1995 when our first store was opened in Plymouth. Today, we have 9 stores, and we are proud to remain a family-owned and operated business.

We are passionate about all pets, and our highly-trained, dedicated staff are pet owners just like you.

Our online store offers over 18,000 quality products for all pets, including dogs, cats, reptiles, birds, small pets, and fish. Our excellent relationships with the industry’s biggest suppliers allow us to offer this wide range of products at great prices.

Our Values

Passion: We are passionate about pets and ensure they are cared for to the highest possible standards. We believe all pets are man's best friend.

Advice: We ensure pet owners buy the right products for their pets' needs. Our trained staff have a broad range of knowledge, so if you aren’t sure about something, please get in touch.

Wellbeing: Relax and trust that we have your pet's best interests at heart. We strive to do everything in our power to ensure your pet is the happiest and healthiest it can be.

Service: We are committed to providing the highest levels of service. Our mission is for Petopia to become your favorite pet shop, and we want you to tell all your friends about us. Judging by all the positive reviews, we hope we’re doing a pretty good job.
